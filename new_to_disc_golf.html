What is disc Golf?

Disc golf is very similar to tradiational golf, except players throw a disc (or frisbee) instead of hitting a ball with a club. Each hole begins with a tee off pad.

Disc Golf Basket

Instead of a hole, disc golf has baskets that the disc must land in. Hitting the chains above the basket is usually suffice to get the disc to drop in the basket.

How Do I Get Started

Stop by Huck It Disc golf at 1525 Newton Rd. Middleton, PEI. No need to book a tee off time, simply walk on and play. Admission is $10. Disc rentals is $5.
