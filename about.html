The Story of Huck It

Huck It was built and designed by Charles McCardle on his family farm's woodlot from 2014-2018. Through many long hours of turmoil and sweat Charles turned his dream into a reality.Friends and family from around the community also helped, contributing sponsorships, labour, and expertise during the construction of Huck It. Huck It extends its gratitude to all of them.

Huck It would also fortunuate to catch the attention of Ben Smith and the Professional Disc Golf Association Canada (PDGA) during its construction phase. The PDGA was planning on hosting the 2018 Canadian National Championship on PEI (from sept. 14-20), and Smith was on the Island making preperations. Smith read about Huck It online, and stopped by to check out Huck It's progress. Though still under construction, Smith was impressed with both Huck It's potiental and Charles's work ethic. In fact Smith was so impressed that he offered Huck It the opportunity to co-host the 2018 Canadian National Championship!

Huck It wasn't scheduled to be completed to until the fall of 2019, but its hard for a new course to turn down an opportunity to host some of the worlds best players. Not wanting to waste such a great opportunity, Smith and Charles began working together to ensure Huck It would be ready in time. Smith's experience and financial support, combined with Charles's tireless work ethic ensured that Huck It got completed by September 2018, just in time for the 2018 National Disc Golf Championship!! Huck It extends a warm thank you to Ben Smith and PDGA Canada for all of your support!!
