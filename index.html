Huck It Disc Golf - Officially Open to the Public

Welcome to Huck it Disc Golf, Prince Edward Island's new Disc Golf Course. While the course is still officially under construction, the course is complete enough for playthroughs. No need to book a tee off time, just stop on by, $10 a round. $2 Disc Rental.
